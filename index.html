About me

  • Currently, I am a Research Engineer at Center for Research on Intelligent Perception and Computing (CRIPAC), Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA), work closely with Prof. Ran He and Prof. Zhenan Sun. Before that I received the B.E. degree in Software Engineering from Dalian University of Technology in 2015 and the M.S. degree in Pattern Recognition and Intelligent System from CASIA in 2018.
  • I am active, creative and self-motivated, with solid knowledge and skills in deep learning and pattern recognition. My research interest lies in face analysis and synthesis, face recognition, knowledge distillation, neural architecture search, generative adversarial network, metric learning etc.
